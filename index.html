Our Strategy

Simply put, Solana Dinero ($SOLDN) charges a flat 1% fee on all buys, and uses over half of that fee to reinvest to the pool, raising it's price floor.

Solana Dinero's Plan

Everything for Solana Dinero ($SOLDN) has been setup to have no maintenance costs. When we receive LP profits we can use them to reinvest to the pool in order to raise the price floor over time. This will consistently raise the minimum value that SOLDN can drop to, so over time, this will create growth (Although we can't guarantee everyone will profit).


Friday Buy Day, starting Friday September 19, we buy at least $1 from personal funds and pay to deposit that back to the pool, we don't sell.

Break Down

  1. Charge 1% fee on buy transaction
  2. Use half of that fee to reinvest
  3. Do nothing else, incur no costs

Work Smarter not Harder! We do nearly nothing, on purpose. Money doesn't have to be complicated, and investments just need people buying them. We have no staff, no overhead, no loans to repay, and no smart contracts to tie up funds, just growth!
